Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

To create an account, click on the "Login/Register" button at the top right corner of the page and follow the instructions.

How do I reset my password?

You can reset your password by clicking on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page and following the instructions sent to your email.

Can I change my username?

Currently, it is not possible to change your username after creating an account. However, you can contact support for assistance.
